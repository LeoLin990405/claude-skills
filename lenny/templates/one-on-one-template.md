## Feedback Framework (SBI)

When giving feedback in 1:1s:

1. **Situation**: "In [specific context]..."
2. **Behavior**: "...I noticed you [specific action]..."
3. **Impact**: "...which resulted in [specific outcome]."
4. Follow up: "What's your perspective?"

**Positive feedback**: Be as specific as critical feedback. "Good job" is not feedback.
**Critical feedback**: Within 48 hours, in private, focused on behavior not character.
