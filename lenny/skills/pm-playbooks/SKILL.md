# PM Playbooks: Role-Based Toolkit Combinations

Each playbook combines PM Toolkit modules into a workflow sequence for a specific role. Follow the modules in order for the recommended progression.

## 1. Startup Founder

**For**: First-time founders, pre-seed to Series A

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-discovery](../pm-discovery/SKILL.md) → Validate the problem exists
2. [pm-strategy](../pm-strategy/SKILL.md) → Define vision, north star, MVP scope
3. [pm-execution](../pm-execution/SKILL.md) → Ship the MVP fast
4. [pm-growth](../pm-growth/SKILL.md) → Find product-market fit
5. [pm-team](../pm-team/SKILL.md) → Hire founding team, run founder sales
6. [pm-leadership](../pm-leadership/SKILL.md) → Fundraise, pivot if needed

**Key Skills**: Problem Definition, Scoping & Cutting, Shipping Products, Measuring PMF, Founder Sales, Fundraising Strategy

---

## 2. First-Time Manager

**For**: ICs transitioning to management

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-team](../pm-team/SKILL.md) → Running 1:1s, delegation, difficult conversations
2. [pm-communication](../pm-communication/SKILL.md) → Written communication, stakeholder alignment
3. [pm-execution](../pm-execution/SKILL.md) → Meetings, decisions, cross-functional work
4. [pm-leadership](../pm-leadership/SKILL.md) → Coaching, energy management, imposter syndrome

**Key Skills**: Running Effective 1:1s, Delegating Work, Having Difficult Conversations, Coaching Product Managers, Energy Management

---

## 3. Product Manager

**For**: PMs at any level

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-discovery](../pm-discovery/SKILL.md) → User interviews, problem definition
2. [pm-strategy](../pm-strategy/SKILL.md) → PRD, OKRs, roadmap, prioritization
3. [pm-execution](../pm-execution/SKILL.md) → Ship, coordinate, decide
4. [pm-communication](../pm-communication/SKILL.md) → Stakeholders, presentations
5. [pm-leadership](../pm-leadership/SKILL.md) → Product taste, systems thinking

**Key Skills**: Conducting User Interviews, Writing PRDs, Prioritizing Roadmap, Shipping Products, Stakeholder Alignment, Developing Product Taste

---

## 4. Growth Leader

**For**: Growth PMs, growth marketers (post-PMF)

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-growth](../pm-growth/SKILL.md) → PMF measurement, growth loops, experiments
2. [pm-discovery](../pm-discovery/SKILL.md) → Surveys, user feedback analysis
3. [pm-analytics](../pm-analytics/SKILL.md) → Metrics trees, cohort analysis
4. [pm-communication](../pm-communication/SKILL.md) → Content marketing, community, positioning
5. [pm-leadership](../pm-leadership/SKILL.md) → AI strategy, behavioral design

**Key Skills**: Designing Growth Loops, Retention & Engagement, Pricing Strategy, User Onboarding, A/B Testing, Content Marketing

---

## 5. Engineering Manager

**For**: Tech leads and EMs

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-execution](../pm-execution/SKILL.md) → Decisions, meetings, design reviews, retros
2. [pm-analytics](../pm-analytics/SKILL.md) → Tech debt management, platform decisions, design systems
3. [pm-team](../pm-team/SKILL.md) → Hiring, 1:1s, onboarding, culture
4. [pm-communication](../pm-communication/SKILL.md) → Stakeholder alignment, offsites

**Key Skills**: Managing Tech Debt, Technical Roadmaps, Conducting Interviews, Running Effective Meetings, Design Systems, Engineering Culture

---

## 6. Sales Leader

**For**: VP Sales, CROs, sales managers

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-team](../pm-team/SKILL.md) → Building sales team, compensation, founder sales, enterprise sales
2. [pm-communication](../pm-communication/SKILL.md) → Presentations, positioning, stakeholder alignment
3. [pm-strategy](../pm-strategy/SKILL.md) → Competitive analysis, north star metrics
4. [pm-growth](../pm-growth/SKILL.md) → Pricing strategy, marketplace dynamics

**Key Skills**: Founder Sales, Enterprise Sales, Sales Compensation Design, Sales Qualification, Building Sales Team, Positioning & Messaging

---

## 7. AI Builder

**For**: AI/ML PMs, AI engineers, AI startup founders

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-leadership](../pm-leadership/SKILL.md) → AI product strategy, building with LLMs, evals
2. [pm-strategy](../pm-strategy/SKILL.md) → PRD, problem definition, scoping
3. [pm-discovery](../pm-discovery/SKILL.md) → User research, usability testing
4. [pm-growth](../pm-growth/SKILL.md) → PMF for AI products, activation
5. [pm-execution](../pm-execution/SKILL.md) → Ship, iterate, design reviews

**Key Skills**: AI Product Strategy, Building with LLMs, AI Evaluation (Evals), Vibe Coding, Evaluating New Technology, Dogfooding

## 8. Executive Communication

**For**: Directors, VPs, C-suite

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-communication](../pm-communication/SKILL.md) → Presentations, writing, media, offsites
2. [pm-leadership](../pm-leadership/SKILL.md) → Org design, coaching, energy management
3. [pm-strategy](../pm-strategy/SKILL.md) → Vision, north star, systems thinking
4. [pm-execution](../pm-execution/SKILL.md) → Decision processes, planning under uncertainty

**Key Skills**: Giving Presentations, Stakeholder Alignment, Media Relations, Organizational Design, Brand Storytelling, Running Offsites

---

## 9. Career Growth

**For**: Anyone advancing their career

**Workflow Sequence**:
1. [pm-leadership](../pm-leadership/SKILL.md) → Imposter syndrome, productivity, startup ideation
2. [pm-communication](../pm-communication/SKILL.md) → Writing, presentations
3. [pm-team](../pm-team/SKILL.md) → Managing up, building a promotion case, finding mentors, negotiation

**Key Skills**: Managing Imposter Syndrome, Personal Productivity, Building a Promotion Case, Career Transitions, Finding Mentors & Sponsors, Written Communication

## Custom Playbooks

Combine any modules for your specific situation:
- "I'm a technical PM at a growth-stage B2B SaaS" → discovery + strategy + analytics + execution
- "I'm a PM transitioning to founder" → leadership (ideation) + discovery + strategy + team (founder sales)
- "I'm an EM moving to product" → discovery + strategy + communication + growth
